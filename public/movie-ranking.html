Best Movies according to Me

Top 3 Movies of all time

Whiplash

Andrew enrols in a music conservatory to become a drummer. But he is mentored by Terence Fletcher, whose unconventional training methods push him beyond the boundaries of reason and sensibility

Forrest Gump

Forrest, a man with low IQ, recounts the early years of his life when he found himself in the middle of key historical events.

Life is beautiful

When his Italian nation is occupied by Nazi German troops during WWII, Guido, an easy-going Jewish librarian, uses willpower, humour and imagination to shield his son from anti-Semitic horrors.
